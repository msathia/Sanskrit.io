Sanskrit Learning Resources

Primary Learning Platforms

Amarahasa

A comprehensive platform for learning Sanskrit through stories and interactive content.

Devanagari Tutor

Interactive platform for learning Devanagari script with writing and sound practice. Includes mobile version for on-the-go learning.

University Courses

UBC Sanskrit (Prof. Adheesh Sathaye)

Comprehensive video series covering Sanskrit grammar and literature. Associated website (ubcsanskrit.ca) offers additional exercises for Sandhi, Declension, and Conjugation.

Cambridge Sanskrit (Prof. A. M. Ruppel)

High-quality video lectures with associated website (cambridge-sanskrit.org) offering additional resources including flashcards and detailed grammar explanations.

University of Chicago (Prof. Andrew Ollett)

Comprehensive course with video lessons and associated grammar resources. Includes detailed lessons and grammar explanations.

Grammar and Reference Tools

Ashtadyayi Online Reference

Digital reference for Panini's Ashtadyayi, including the first auspicious sutra and detailed grammatical rules.

Sanskrit Grammar for Vedanta Students

Comprehensive grammar resource focusing on Sandhi rules and their application in Vedanta studies.

Additional Learning Resources

Aksharamukha Converter

Tool for converting between different Sanskrit scripts, created by Dr. Vinodh Rajan.

Sanskrit Abhyas

Interactive platform for learning Sanskrit vocabulary and grammar, including pronoun forms and word structures.

Madras Sanskrit College

Digital campus offering various Sanskrit courses and learning materials.

NPTEL Courses

Introduction to Spoken Sanskrit (Basic)

Basic level course by Prof. Anuradha Choudry covering fundamental Sanskrit speaking skills.

Introduction to Spoken Sanskrit (Intermediate)

Intermediate level course building upon basic Sanskrit speaking skills.
